SELF-SOVEREIGN IDENTITY

ZADA IDENTITY NETWORK

Driven to ensure everyone has equal opportunities to benefit from new digital services and offerings in a safe way without risk for fraud or privacy intrusion, ZADA provides decentralized identity ecosystems where the user owns his/her identity and manages the identity data received from organizations and decides if to share with others.

ZADA Network uses blockchain to enable a secure exchange of personal data and identities inline with the concept of self-sovereign identity and serves as the foundation for the ZADA Ecosystem. The Ecosystem is to create a Web of Trust — a decentralized platform of trust interconnecting Identity Owners and the Things they control. There is a Governance Framework (ZEGF) that defines the business, legal, and technical policies for the ZADA Ecosystem and an Ecosystem Governance Board is to administer governance for the ZADA Ecosystem on behalf of all Identity Owners. For more details about ZADA ecosystem contact us.

VACCIFY

Vaccify is a blockchain-based digital identity solution for all healthcare institutes, laboratories, and testing facilities. Its purpose is to enable them to directly send digitally signed credentials containing people’s COVID-19 vaccination status to their smartphones. It also facilitates the governance of vaccination passporting by allowing the institutions like aviation authorities or commercial airlines to verify people’s vaccination status, ultimately helping society to return to the norm and function in a seamless manner. In a traditional approach such a solution would end up with thousands of integration pipelines increasing cost and performance risks. The solution is deliberately based on Self-Sovereign Identity (SSI).

An SSI-based digital credential surpasses paper credentials because of obvious advantages offered by the blockchain technology. These digital credentials have the ability to maintain the data confidentiality of people innovatively. It enables users to prove specific facts about them without sharing data digitally. It would cryptographically verify their vaccination status without revealing any personal information to 3rd party services. This kind of digital identity puts the user as the focal point of services. Providing people with more autonomy over their digital identity opens doors for new user-centric and privacy-oriented services. It can also enable prior services to be faster, secure, and portable while placing a tab on nonconsensual data harnessing. For more details about Vaccify contact us.

ZADA WALLET

ZADA Wallet is the digital ID wallet that puts you in control over your identities online. ZADA Wallet allows its users to store and manage digitally verifiable credentials on your phone. With ZADA Wallet, people can interact with organizations like never before, instantly proving who they are while also protecting their privacy. All of the credentials are stored only on the device itself, giving people full control over what credentials they share and with whom.

ZADA Wallet allows its users use the ZADA ecosystem and ZADA is on a mission to ensure that everyone has equal opportunities to benefit from new digital services and offerings, ZADA is a provider of digital identity ecosystems. Within the ZADA Network, organizations and individuals can securely share personal data through digital credentials. Not only can the organizations issue digital credentials of all kinds to their customers (e.g., passport, driver’s license, credit score, proof of employment, education degree, etc.), but any organization within the ZADA Network can request and instantly verify the information contained in those credentials.

ONEHYPERNET

OneHypernet connects the world’s foreign exchange markets – banks, corporates, and payment service providers on a unified ecosystem powered by enterprise blockchain technology. It has been awarded the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS)’s Financial Sector Technology and Innovation (FSTI) grant. OneHypernet uses enterprise decentralized technology to connect markets on a global ledger, enabling a real-time shared view with standardized protocols and data privacy. When privacy is preserved, any and all users can be connected. Instead of searching through a chain of banking agents, the most efficient payment route is generated from all permutations, givins customers direct access to local accounts.

OneHypernet uses confidential computing to aggregate all transactions in a secure enclave. This means that data can be processed without being accessed by anyone, not even by OHN. This provides a complete view of the market and achieves the most efficient prices possible. OHN decentralized netting technology saves FX participants 96% of their cross-border fees while shortening the payment duration. For more details about OneHypernet contact us.

DEFI & ASSET TOKENIZATION

OMAXX

OMAXX is a first of its kind decentralized equity crowdfunding autonomous organization (DAO). It enables startups to raise cryptos to fund their startups and enables investors to invest in cutting-edge unique startups securely. OMAXX aims to build a blockchain-based secondary market platform to address equity crowdfunding post-investment issues. The core activities are the tokenization of shares, the listing of tokenized shares, pricing of shares, matching of buyers to sellers, buying and selling of tokenized shares, share transfers, currency transfer, clearing and settlement, voting, monitoring and management of the governance, expenditure, compensation of entrepreneurs and managers post-investment. OMAXX utilizes several ERC-20 smart contracts and also includes custom tokenomics & business logic. The decentralized application (DApp) also has metamask and moralis wallet integration.
