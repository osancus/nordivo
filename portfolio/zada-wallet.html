About ZADA Wallet

In today's fast-paced digital world, having control over your online identities is of utmost importance. That's why we are thrilled to present ZADA Wallet, a cutting-edge digital ID wallet that puts you in command of your credentials. With ZADA Wallet, you can securely store and manage digitally verifiable credentials right on your phone, revolutionizing the way you interact with organizations and protect your privacy.

In the realm of digital identities, the challenge was clear – creating a solution that not only allowed seamless interaction with organizations but also ensured uncompromised data privacy and security. ZADA Wallet aimed to give users full control over their credentials, enabling them to choose what to share and with whom. The task was to build a platform that offered equal opportunities to all, fostering a secure ecosystem for digital services and offerings.

what we crafted

Design & Solution

Working in close collaboration with ZADA, our team at Nordivo developed a comprehensive solution based on state-of-the-art technology. Harnessing the power of Hyperledger Indy, Hyperledger Aries, and Hyperledger Ursa, along with advanced cryptography and the Sovrin Network, we crafted an ecosystem that guarantees secure and efficient sharing of personal data through digital credentials.

The implementation of ZADA Mobile Wallet involved the development of a sophisticated and user-friendly mobile application. Our goal was to create a seamless user experience, enabling individuals to effortlessly manage their digital identities. By incorporating the power of Hyperledger technology, cryptography, and the Sovrin Network, all credentials are securely stored on the user's device, putting the control of data sharing firmly in their hands.

Result

Final Outcomes

With ZADA Mobile Wallet, users can confidently interact with organizations, instantly verifying their identities while safeguarding their privacy. The platform's architecture ensures that all credentials remain solely on the user's device, providing unparalleled control over shared information and interactions with recipients.

ZADA Mobile Wallet is part of the larger ZADA Network, empowering organizations to issue various digital credentials to their customers. From passports and driver's licenses to credit scores, proof of employment, education degrees, and more, ZADA Mobile Wallet facilitates seamless and secure exchanges within the network.
