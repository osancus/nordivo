About ZADA Ecosystem

ZADA’s objective was to create a secure, efficient, and scalable ecosystem for storing and managing digitally verifiable credentials, enabling users to interact with organizations like never before, instantly proving who they are while also protecting their privacy. One of the main challenges was finding a way to securely share personal data through digital credentials, without compromising data privacy or security. Additionally, ZADA Ecosystem sought to provide users with full control over what credentials they share and with whom.

Design & Solution

Nordivo worked with ZADA to develop a custom solution based on Hyperledger technology, including Hyperledger Indy, Hyperledger Aries, and Hyperledger Ursa. This enabled secure sharing of personal data through digital credentials, while preserving data privacy and security. The solution used cryptography and the Sovrin Network to ensure that all credentials were stored only on the device itself, giving people full control over what credentials they share and with whom. Nordivo used React Native, ReactJS, NodeJS, Express, MongoDB, Heroku, and Amazon Web Services (AWS) to develop the ZADA Ecosystem.

Nordivo's implementation of the ZADA Ecosystem involved the development of sophisticated web applications, scalable APIs with seamless blockchain integrations, and a cross-platform mobile application (Wallet app) for enhanced accessibility. To ensure robust performance and scalability, the cloud infrastructure was built on both AWS and Heroku, delivering a reliable and efficient platform for users worldwide.

Utilizing Hyperledger technology, including Hyperledger Indy, Hyperledger Aries, and Hyperledger Ursa, the solution ensures a seamless and secure experience for sharing personal information. Additionally, cryptography and the Sovrin Network guarantee that all credentials remain solely on the user's device, providing individuals full control over their shared credentials and recipients.
